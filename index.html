About Me

I am a part-time PhD student at the Department of Statistics, Imperial College, London. I am supervised by Prof. Ciara Pike-Burke. I am also a part-time data scientist at The Trade Desk AI lab in London.

I am interested in sequential decision making problems in noisy environments such as in computational advertising. Previously, I received a masters degree in Operations Research from Columbia University, USA, and a bachelors degree from R. V. College of Engineering, Bangalore, India.
